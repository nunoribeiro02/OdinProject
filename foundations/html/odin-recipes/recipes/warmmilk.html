Warm Milk

Milk! You almost certainly have it in your fridge. Now, the question is: what is the best way to drink it?

Behold: the famous warm milk recipe!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Microwave

Recipe Instructions

  1. Take your cold milk out of the fridge.
  2. Pick up a cup, mug or tea cup if you are feeling fancy.
  3. Place your chosen cup on the counter.
  4. Pour milk into it.
  5. Put your milk cup in the microwave
  6. Set the timer to somewhere between 40 seconds to 1 minute and 30 seconds.
  7. Wait.
  8. When the timer stops and you hear a beep, open the microwave.
  9. Take your milk cup out of the microwave (be careful not to drop it)!
  10. The milk is now warm and ready to consume (if you are not lactose intolerant).
  11. Enjoy!
