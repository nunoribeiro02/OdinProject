Bread and Butter

Do you like bread? Who doesn't! Do you wonder what more do do with it? Everyone does!

I present you a simple and delicious recipe that everyone can make: Bread and Butter!

Ingridients

  • Bread
  • Butter

Recipe Instructions

  1. Pick up a bread knife.
  2. Slice your bread, if not slice already.
  3. Open your butter container.
  4. Pick up a smaller knife.
  5. Spread the butter on the sliced bread.
  6. And it's ready to serve. Enjoy!
